What is a dongle and how it works?

A dongle plugs into your laptop's USB port and then acts as a portable modem. Once plugged in, the dongle connects to the internet in the same way as your smartphone and gives the user 3G, 4G or 5G connectivity.

What is a data card?

A data card or a smart card is a removable computer component that contains information. It is used for data operations, such as input, output, storage, transfer, and transformation.

How many devices can be connected to VoizFonica dongle?

VoizFonicaFi device allows multiple users and mobile devices to access VoizFonica's 4G high-speed internet connectivity and create a personal Wi-Fi hotspot. The LYF-powered VoizFonicaFi device can connect a minimum of 10 devices + 1 USB connection, with some models having the ability to support many more connections.

Can VoizFonica dongle be used as Wi-Fi?

A VoizFonica dongle, hotspot, or data card is an excellent device to convert any place into your own personal Wi-Fi zone. These devices come with different prepaid and postpaid offers and plans to get the internet services that you are looking for.

How can I speed up my VoizFonica dongle?

1. Go to the browser and visit the VoizFonicafi login page. 2.Enter your valid username and password. 3.Then select Setting > LTE > APN from list. 4.Now change the APN network from automatic to manual. 5.The final step is to switch your IP address to IPV4 and then click the Apply setting button.
